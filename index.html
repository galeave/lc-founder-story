About Me

I'm Eleza, a product leader who has spent over 20 years building software, apps, and digital experiences.

Alongside my career in tech, I've spent much of my life practicing martial arts:

  • Black belt in Goju Ryu Karate
  • Several years of kickboxing
  • Early experience in BJJ

Training didn't make me a professional fighter, but it shaped how I see the sport — the discipline, repetition, conditioning, injuries, mental toughness, and quiet resilience that fighters live with every day.

What I'm Building (and Why)

The deeper I train and follow combat sports, the more one reality stands out:

Fighters are being pushed to do more — far beyond training and competing.

To increase their earning potential, many fighters take on additional work: content creation, personal branding, merch, and multiple platforms — all while preparing for fights and staying competitive.

At the same time, die-hard fans want to support fighters, especially those who give everything inside the cage but don't always receive the financial upside.

Yet there is still no simple, trusted, direct way for fans to financially support a fighter without friction or middlemen.

This gap is why LevelChange exists.

Mission

Our mission is to help fighters reach their full potential through direct fan support.

LevelChange gives fighters another path — a way to earn without extra work, and a way for fans to support what matters most: the athlete's craft, discipline, and performance.

How It Works
