What Is LevelChange?

A simple, secure tipping platform for combat sports.

LevelChange creates a clean, direct way for fans to support fighters — quickly and meaningfully.

  • Direct financial support for fighters
  • Quick, secure tipping for fans
  • No algorithms or extra content
  • Built specifically for martial artists and combat sports

This is appreciation → directly to the athlete.

How It Works for Fighters

  1. Create your LevelChange profile
  2. Verify your payout account
  3. Share your link to receive tips instantly

Fighters receive 100% of tips. Low fees are covered by fans. Payments are processed through Stripe for security, compliance and fraud protection.

How It Works for Fans

  1. Search for fighters or use a link/QR
  2. Choose an amount
  3. Support your fighter instantly

Low fees are added to the tip to cover secure payment processing and platform maintenance. Payments are processed through Stripe for security, compliance and fraud protection.

Simple. Secure. Direct.

Mission

Our mission is to help fighters reach their full potential through direct fan support.

LevelChange gives fighters another path — a way to earn without extra work, and a way for fans to support what matters most: the athlete's craft, discipline, and performance.

Founder
